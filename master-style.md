# Master Design System — Tokyo Shibuya Night Streetwear Marketplace

Design a premium web application for a **streetwear thrift marketplace** inspired by the vibrant nightlife of **Tokyo Shibuya**. The interface should combine modern minimalism with the energetic atmosphere of illuminated city streets, premium fashion boutiques, and curated vintage culture.

This application is intended to feel like a modern fashion startup rather than a generic online marketplace.

---

# Brand Identity

The brand should communicate:

* Premium
* Fashion-forward
* Modern
* Urban
* Curated
* Youthful
* Sophisticated
* Streetwear
* Japanese-inspired
* Vintage
* Lifestyle
* Editorial

Imagine walking through **Shibuya Crossing at night**, surrounded by glowing storefronts, luxury fashion boutiques, illuminated billboards, cafés, and rain-soaked streets reflecting colorful city lights.

The experience should feel immersive, stylish, and welcoming.

# Motion & Animation

Animations are an essential part of the experience.

The motion should feel inspired by Apple, Linear, Framer, and modern startup websites.

Never use flashy or exaggerated animations.

### Page Transition

* Fade in
* Soft upward movement
* 300–500ms
* Smooth ease-out

### Navbar

* Smooth transparency while scrolling
* Blur increases on scroll
* Active menu transitions smoothly
* Animated underline

### Buttons

Hover:

* Scale 1.02–1.05
* Soft accent glow
* Smooth transition

Click:

* Gentle press animation

### Cards

Hover:

* Lift slightly
* Shadow increases
* Image zooms 3–5%
* Additional information fades in

### Images

* Fade in on load
* Slight zoom on hover

### Scroll Reveal

Every major section should animate into view with:

* Fade
* Upward movement
* Staggered animation for grids

### Hero Section

Reveal order:

1. Headline
2. Subtitle
3. Primary CTA
4. Secondary CTA
5. Hero image

### Loading States

Use elegant skeleton loaders.

Buttons show subtle loading indicators.

### Performance

Animations should remain smooth at 60 FPS and never reduce usability.
